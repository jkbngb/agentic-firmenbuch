Subscription

Manage or cancel subscription

Enter your e-mail address. We send you a link to the Stripe customer portal, where you can cancel your Pro subscription at any time, change your payment method or view your invoices.

For security reasons, the link goes exclusively to your inbox – so only the holder of the address can manage the subscription. You keep full access until the end of the paid period; after that access switches to Free automatically – nothing further is charged.

  1. Enter your e-mail address and click "Request management link".
  2. Open the link in the e-mail – you land directly in the Stripe customer portal.
  3. Choose "Cancel subscription" there. Done – access stays until the end of the paid period, then switches to Free automatically.
